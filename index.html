Field Scientific Event

Astronomy Course

📍 Scientific Entertainment Center, Sidi Bel Abbes

A comprehensive theoretical and practical workshop in the field of space sciences and astronomy. It started with a theoretical explanation of the beginning of human friction with space, followed by a break for the participants, then the practical workshop began with a full introduction to the solar system and astronomical phenomena, and concluded with explaining the mechanism of the telescope.

Digital & Technological Management

Managing SHIELD DZ Summit

📍 Tlemcen - Within DZ Young Leaders Program

Tomouh Club took over the full technical management of the SHIELD DZ project launch, which included:

  • Designing smart cards for attendance registration via QR Code scanning.
  • A dedicated digital platform for receiving questions during live lectures.
  • An advanced interface for managing the medical check-up system for participants.
  • Building the complete prototype (MVP) of the project idea and presenting it at the closing.

About The Club

Our mission, identity, and official headquarters

Tomouh Club is founded at the Scientific Entertainment Center in the state of Sidi Bel Abbes (Mascara Road). The club is interested in organizing forums and adding innovative technical touches, and is also the founder of "Tomouh Podcast" directed specifically to Baccalaureate students. The club is built by young hands; they are outstanding university students from various states who believed in one idea: "Let us be the beginning.. and let our ambition be a success story".
